About Me

From Markets to Machines

My journey began with mastering liquidity-based price action in the Forex markets, where I learned that precision beats prediction. Every trade taught me patience, discipline, and the art of calculated risk.

Today, I bridge the gap between traditional trading and cutting-edge AI. I build tools that help struggling Pakistani traders access the same institutional-grade analysis that was once exclusive to the elite. My mission is simple: democratize financial intelligence.

Currently pursuing data science while developing fintech solutions, I believe the future belongs to those who can blend market intuition with machine learning precision.
